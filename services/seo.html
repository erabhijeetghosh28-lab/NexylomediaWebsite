SEO & Content Strategy for Growth That Compounds

We help lifestyle and design-led brands grow organic traffic and conversions with technical SEO, on-page optimization and authority-building content. Every month, we prioritize actions that move you closer to commercial keywords, not vanity wins.

What’s Included

  • Technical SEO audit & fixes (indexing, Core Web Vitals, schema, sitemaps, redirects)
  • On-page optimization (titles, meta, internal links, intent match, UX)
  • Keyword research & topic clusters mapped to the buyer journey
  • Editorial calendar & conversion-ready content (service pages, guides, FAQs)
  • Authority building via digital PR / outreach and link-worthy assets
  • Monthly reporting with GSC + GA4 and clear next actions

Our SEO Process

  1. Audit & Tracking — Crawl health, Core Web Vitals, schema, analytics and GSC setup.
  2. Strategy — Keyword map, topical clusters, content briefs and internal link blueprint.
  3. Optimize & Publish — On-page fixes, content production, UX polish, interlinking.
  4. Measure & Iterate — Monthly reports with CTR tests, new content, and link outreach.
